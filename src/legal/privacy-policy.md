# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 10, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Mpala Study ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("Mpala Study" or the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, university name, year of study
- **Authentication Data:** Encrypted password (we never store plain-text passwords)
- **Study Data:** Study plans, course names, exam dates, study session records
- **Financial Information:** Wallet balances, transaction history within the app
- **Communication Data:** Messages sent to our AI coach (Japha AI)

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** App features used, time spent on screens, session duration
- **Log Data:** Access times, error logs, diagnostic information

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- Location data (GPS)
- Contact lists
- Camera or microphone access (unless explicitly enabled by you)
- Biometric data
- Third-party social media data

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

- **Service Delivery:** Creating and managing your account, providing study planning features
- **Personalization:** Customizing AI coaching recommendations based on your study patterns
- **Analytics:** Understanding app usage to improve features and user experience
- **Communication:** Sending notifications about study sessions, reminders, and important updates
- **Security:** Detecting and preventing fraud, unauthorized access, and other malicious activities
- **Legal Compliance:** Meeting regulatory requirements and responding to lawful requests

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Security Measures

- All passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt encryption
- Data transmission is encrypted using TLS/SSL
- JWT tokens are used for secure authentication with expiration controls
- Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

### 4.2 Data Retention

- Account data is retained while your account is active
- Upon account deletion, personal data is removed within 30 days
- Anonymized analytics data may be retained for statistical purposes

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data only:

- **With Your Consent:** When you explicitly authorize sharing
- **Service Providers:** Trusted partners who help operate our app (hosting, analytics)
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law, court order, or government request
- **Safety:** To protect the rights, safety, and property of users and others

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction:** Update inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a portable format
- **Withdrawal:** Withdraw consent for optional data processing
- **Objection:** Object to certain data processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@mpalastudy.com

## 7. Children's Privacy

Mpala Study is intended for users aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

## 9. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes through the App or via email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
