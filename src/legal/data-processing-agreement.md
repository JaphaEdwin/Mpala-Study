# Data Processing Agreement

**Last Updated: March 10, 2026**

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") forms part of the Terms of Service between you ("User," "Data Subject") and Mpala Study ("Data Controller," "we," "us").

## 1. Definitions

- **Personal Data:** Any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual
- **Processing:** Any operation performed on Personal Data (collection, storage, use, disclosure, deletion)
- **Data Controller:** Mpala Study, which determines the purposes and means of processing
- **Data Processor:** Third parties who process data on our behalf
- **Data Subject:** The individual to whom Personal Data relates (you)

## 4. Your Data Subject Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR and local regulations), you have:

### 4.1 Right of Access
Request confirmation of whether we process your data and obtain a copy.

### 4.2 Right to Rectification
Request correction of inaccurate personal data.

### 4.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
Request deletion of your data when:
- Data is no longer necessary for original purpose
- You withdraw consent
- You object to processing
- Data was unlawfully processed

### 4.4 Right to Restrict Processing
Request limitation of processing under certain circumstances.

### 4.5 Right to Data Portability
Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

### 4.6 Right to Object
Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.

### 4.7 Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making
Request human review of decisions made solely by automated means that significantly affect you.

## 5. How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights:

1. **In-App:** Settings → Privacy → Data Requests
2. **Email:** privacy@mpalastudy.com
3. **Written Request:** Include your registered email and specific request

We will respond within 30 days. Complex requests may require an additional 60 days with notice.

When data is transferred outside your country of residence:

## 8. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:

**Technical:**
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
- Regular security assessments and penetration testing
- Access logging and monitoring

**Organizational:**
- Staff training on data protection
- Access controls based on need-to-know
- Incident response procedures
- Regular policy reviews

## 9. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach:
- We will assess the risk to your rights and freedoms
- If high risk, we will notify you without undue delay
- We will inform relevant supervisory authorities within 72 hours where required
- We will document all breaches and response actions

## 10. Children's Data

We do not knowingly process data of individuals under 16. If you believe a child's data has been collected, contact us immediately for removal.

## 11. Consent Management

Where processing is based on consent:
- Consent is freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous
- You may withdraw consent at any time
- Withdrawal does not affect processing done before withdrawal
- We provide easy mechanisms to withdraw consent
